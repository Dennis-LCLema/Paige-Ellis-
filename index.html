Paige Ellis

BIO

Bringing a refreshing reminder of past R&B sounds, Paige Ellis is an artist on the rise. Influences and sounds similar to those of Brandy and Monica, Paige is able to make music with respect of her inspirations while creating her own unique sound and path.

— Somebody
Paige Ellis discovered her passion for singing after casting a role in a musical in 8th grade. Throughout high school, she performed in talent shows and was recognized by many as the one who would “most likely be famous.” Despite her shy and introverted demeanor, she has made great leaps and sacrifices to pursue her passion for music.
Paige released her first single “Better” in 2017 offering a soulful sound referencing an emotional experience of a breakup. She showed off her dancing skills with her 2018 single and music video “Old Love” bringing a 90’s party vibe to the scene. With the release of her first EP titled F.E.A.R.S. , she is able to tell more of her personal story about common fears she faces when it comes to love, life, and acceptance. "
